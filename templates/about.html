About Us

Hi my name is Rishi Zarkar and I am a sophomore at the University of Maryland

I started this Terps4Turtles to bring awareness to the Galapagos Giant Tortoises which are an endangered species due to habitat destruction and destruction from invasive species. This web app allows users to interact individually with the species and give each user a personal experience through the interactive UI. I hope that this web application helps raise money to supportorganizations that will help the Giant Tortoise population recover.
